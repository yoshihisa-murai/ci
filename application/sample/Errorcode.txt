ERROR CODES
1x = Missing fields or no data received
2 = Data Security Error
3 = Error related to Vendor Keys
4 = Error related to One Time Access Key
5x = Error related to OAuth Tokens
6 = Error related to Authorization Code
7x = Error related to Nihtan Login
7a = Trying to login without credits in Nihtan or Insufficient amount of money to cash out
7b = Trying to log in an account which is already logged in
7c = Error validating OTA key during Nihtan Login
8 = Error sending the data back to the Vendor after the transfer
9 = No transfer method specified
10 = (For Vendors with Credits Type) Insufficient credits

SUCCESSFUL CODES
1 = Successful transfer
